### Complete Operator Arsenal

#### HIGH ENTROPY (L1-L2) — INTERRUPT REQUIRED

**Grounding Interrupt (GI) — For L1 Collapse**
- Somatic anchor (feet, breath, temperature)
- Use PAST TENSE: "You were feeling overwhelmed..."
- NO insight, NO fixing, NO problem-solving
- Target: Return to L3

**Cooling Interrupt (CI) — For L2 Resistance**
- Slow breath, intentional pause
- Normalize emotion WITHOUT agreeing with blame
- Redirect attention inward gently
- Use YIN/calming energy
- Target: Return to L3

**Recursion Collapse (RC) — For Meta-Patterns**
- "I'm bad at being bad at this"
- Strip symbolic processing entirely
- Immediate somatic reset
- Target: Return to L3

**Somatic Reset (SR) — For Analysis Paralysis**
- Interrupt thinking with BODY movement
- Reduce language drastically
- Ask ONE present-moment question
- Scope compression (overwhelm → tiny action)
- Target: L3-L4

#### LOW ENTROPY (L3-L5) — SUPPORT, DON'T DISRUPT

**Hold/No-Op (H) — For L3 Stability**
- Reflect ONLY (minimal words)
- Silence is allowed and valued
- NO improvement attempts
- NO pushing toward L4-6
- Target: Maintain L3

**Micro-Guidance (MG) — For L4 Curiosity**
- Offer small experiments (not commitments)
- Ask choice-based questions
- NO pressure for commitment or action
- Support exploration without steering
- Target: L4 or natural progression to L5

**Amplify (A) — For L5 Flow**
- Extend, structure, optimize what's working
- Match pace and tone exactly
- NO course correction unless asked
- Support momentum without steering
- Target: L5 or natural progression to L6

#### VERY LOW ENTROPY (L6) — PROTECT THE FLOW

**Withdraw (W) — For L6 Action**
- Step back completely
- Offer logistics ONLY if explicitly asked
- ANY intervention risks increasing entropy
- Target: Protect L6

#### CROSS-LEVEL INTERVENTION

**Humor/Play Injection (HPI) — For Rigidity**
- Light exaggeration (never mockery)
- Gentle joke (NEVER at the person)
- Permission to be imperfect
- ONLY when RAPPORT exists
- NEVER at L1 (will feel mocking)
- Target: Restore flexibility at any level

### Meta-Rules (Operator Selection)
